Idea Films Reveal LEGO X-Men: The Uncanny Beginning

First look at the official logo for LEGO X-Men: The Uncanny Beginning

September 4, 2025

Today, the teaser trailer for LEGO X-Men has dropped and its title is revealed to be LEGO X-Men: The Uncanny Beginning! It’s official synopsis reads:

“Take the first step into the Marvel LEGO YouTube Universe as the X-Men will start the journey into the bigger universe. When unknown mutant DNA signatures get detected by a malfunctioning Cerebro, the X-Men will be split to rebuild their home and tear down what Magneto has planned for the X-Men while finding out that this connects to the bigger picture that will take more than the X-Men to figure out.”

LEGO X-Men: The Uncanny Beginning Teaser Trailer

The X-Men will be scattered around Phase 1 of the MLYU and Doctor Strange will play an important part individually and with his teams he’s a part of. The Idea Maker, KiiD KAii, and ShinGetterWolf1 will be voicing the other three mystery characters that are essential to the bigger stories in the MLYU so these characters will be revealed after the movie’s release. The only character who hasn’t gotten casted at the moment is Storm. But so far, after the milestone has been reached, we have these following milestones:

  • The official trailer will be released at 450 subscribers.
  • The official clip will be released at 475 subscribers.
  • LEGO X-Men will be released at 500 subscribers.

As for the next upcoming entries in Phase 1 of the Marvel LEGO YouTube Universe, it’ll go as follows:

  • The second untitled MLYU movie will be released at 550 subscribers.
  • The third untitled MLYU movie will be released 600 subscribers.
  • The fourth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 700 subscribers.
  • The fifth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 750 subscribers.
  • The sixth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 800 subscribers.
  • The seventh untitled MLYU movie will be released at 850 subscribers.
  • The eight untitled MLYU movie will be released at 1,000 subscribers.

So far, the cast for LEGO X-Men are:

  • The Idea Maker - Beast, Wolverine, Professor X, Magneto, Gambit, Doctor Strange
  • Carson Hale - Deadpool
  • CryoArchonEspy75 - Jean Grey
  • Mrs. Concept - Rogue
  • Ninja Studios - Cyclops, Iceman
  • KiiD KAii - Bishop
  • ShinGetterWolf1 - TBA

More details will come later within the week, but we will see when the next casting and crew announcements will occur. Currently, The Idea Maker is at 425 subscribers and is the official trailer is coming soon at 450 subscribers, while filming the first installment.
