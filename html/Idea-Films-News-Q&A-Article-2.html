Idea Films News' Q&A Article #2

A LEGO Minifigure

September 6, 2025

With these past articles, there has been many new impressions over at Idea Films News and The Idea Maker’s YouTube channel and another question came in to be answered. This question from friend and YouTuber, Ezra Carr376 who asks:

Ezra Carr376: How many characters will you include in each movie?
The Idea Maker: That’s a great question! It depends on the story and how it can tie into the main story and what and how the plots can build on top of each other without having some sort of discrepancy or plot hole. At the moment, for X-Men: The Uncanny Beginning, we have 17 characters. The next one we have for Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt has 16 characters. Our last one in Phase 1 has approximately 53-55 characters and POSSIBLY counting, but we’ll see where we can take things to wrap up Phase 1.

Even though there was only one question so far, this is a great step towards more Q&A sessions in the future. As a reminder, the timeline consists of these films so far:

  • The official trailer for LEGO X-Men: The Uncanny Beginning will be released at 450 subscribers.
  • The official clip will be released at 475 subscribers.
  • LEGO X-Men: The Uncanny Beginning will be released at 500 subscribers.

As for the next upcoming entries in Phase 1 of the Marvel LEGO YouTube Universe, it’ll go as follows:

  • LEGO X-Men: The Uncanny Beginning will be released at 500 subscribers.
  • LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt will be released at 550 subscribers.
  • The third untitled MLYU movie will be released 600 subscribers.
  • The fourth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 700 subscribers.
  • The fifth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 750 subscribers.
  • The sixth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 800 subscribers.
  • The seventh untitled MLYU movie will be released at 850 subscribers.
  • The eight untitled MLYU movie will be released at 1,000 subscribers.

More details will come later, but we will see when the next Q&A article will be, but I believe that we can have this once a week so that way we can have more questions for the upcoming Q&A article. Currently, The Idea Maker is at 428 subscribers and is working on the teaser trailer/title reveal, while filming the first installment.
