LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt's Script Is OFFICIALLY FINISHED

A Prototype of the official logo for LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt

September 7, 2025

Today, we unveil that the next entry, LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire: Hunt has its script officially written and completed. The timeline for Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt goes as follows:

  • On August 19, 2025, the idea of a Spider-Man movie came into fruition, but not the story. But this got to forming based on a set that will be already pre-built.
  • On August 20, 2025, the script was being secretly being written simultaneously with the LEGO X-Men: The Uncanny Beginning project.
  • On August 30, 2025, Idea Films revealed that a second entry in the MLYU would be coming to the MLYU at 550 subscribers.
  • On September 5, 2025, Idea Films revealed the title of the next MLYU entry, which will be LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt along with its cast so far. There is still in need of a few more characters that need to be casted, but we’ll see where things will go.
  • On September 7, 2025, the script for LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt has been officially finished being written and it will be filmed right after LEGO X-Men: The Uncanny Beginning.
“It’s interesting how this Spider-Man movie is going to bring in the Street-Level, Monster-Level, Mutant-Level, Mystic-Level, AND Cosmic-Level side of the existing Marvel universe and will throw in some amazing character dynamics and hopefully some interactions fans would like to see next! And it’s such an honor to get these voice actors take on the roles of these characters and bring their own faithful spin to these beloved characters!”

- The Idea Maker

As we mentioned before, the X-Men will be scattered around Phase 1 of the MLYU and in this story, we have one X-Men involved. To get those interested in the next entry, we will unveil the title and characters involved in this next entry. We call the next entry, LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt.

For this release, we have these following proposed milestones:

  • The official trailer will be released at 525 subscribers.
  • LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt will be released at 550 subscribers.

As for the next upcoming entries in Phase 1 of the Marvel LEGO YouTube Universe, it’ll go as follows:

  • LEGO X-Men: The Uncanny Beginning will be released at 500 subscribers.
  • LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt will be released at 550 subscribers.
  • The third untitled MLYU movie will be released 600 subscribers.
  • The fourth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 700 subscribers.
  • The fifth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 750 subscribers.
  • The sixth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 800 subscribers.
  • The seventh untitled MLYU movie will be released at 850 subscribers.
  • The eight untitled MLYU movie will be released at 1,000 subscribers.

So far, the cast for LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt are:

  • KiiD KAii - Miles Morales/Spider-Man
  • ReelHooperKnowledge - Peter Parker/Spider-Man
  • The Idea Maker - Morbius, Beast, Doctor Strange, Loki
  • Mikeballer24 - Blade
  • ShinGetterWolf1 - Werewolf By Night
  • Mrs. Concept - Wanda Maximoff

This is the confirmed cast so far. More details will come later within the week, but we will see when the next casting and crew announcements will occur. Currently, The Idea Maker is at 428 subscribers and is the official trailer is coming soon at 450 subscribers, while filming the first installment.
