Idea Films Reveals The Third MLYU Entry

The Marvel Logo

November 8, 2025

Today, we unveil the next entry after LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt. Say hello to the third entry in the LEGO Marvel YouTube Universe.

“For the third entry in the MLYU, we will be focusing on the next chapter in the major story that will be shaping what is going on in this Marvel universe, who the big bad might be, and maybe many teases to where this story is heading. But there is a major story that is going to be shaping and taking over this story. The MLYU is going towards a story that maybe you might know where it’s going, but it’ll be done in a way where you might never have thought of before! This story goes beyond the standard solo team movie, because it’s a team up movie. It focuses on another group, but will team up with the main group and you’ll be amazed at how this could be somewhat of a future collaborative team that’ll be around for a while.”

- The Idea Maker

As we mentioned before, the X-Men will be scattered around Phase 1 of the MLYU and in this story, we have one X-Men involved. To get those interested in the third entry, we will unveil the title and characters involved in this third entry. We call the third entry, LEGO The Fantastic Four: Galactic Pathways.

The Logo for Marvel Studios' The Fantastic Four: First Steps used as inspiration for the MLYU's Fantastic Four movie

For this release, we have these following proposed milestones:

  • The official trailer will be released at 575 subscribers.
  • * LEGO The Fantastic Four: Galactic Pathways will be released at 600 subscribers.

As for the next upcoming entries in Phase 1 of the Marvel LEGO YouTube Universe, it’ll go as follows:

  • LEGO X-Men: The Uncanny Beginning will be released at 500 subscribers.
  • LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt will be released at 550 subscribers.
  • LEGO The Fantastic Four: Galactic Pathways will be released 600 subscribers.
  • The fourth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 650 subscribers.
  • The fifth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 700 subscribers.
  • The sixth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 750 subscribers.
  • The seventh untitled MLYU movie will be released at 800 subscribers.
  • The eighth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 850 subscribers.
  • The ninth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 900 subscribers.
  • The teneth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 950 subscribers.
  • The eleventh untitled MLYU movie will be released at 1,000 subscribers.

So far, the cast for LEGO The Fantastic Four: Galactic Pathways are:

  • ShinGetterWolf1 - Mr. Fantastic

  • ReelHooperKnowledge - Peter Parker/Spider-Man

This is the confirmed cast and the mid credits and post credits characters have also been casted! This is a great start as our casts have been getting more and more solidified the more that we write, talk, film, and formulate the stories and ideas. At the moment, the initial draft is complete and is undergoing proofreading, continuity checks, and story structure checks before the next entry will be written. There is talk of a possible slate reveal of where this story is going to go, in which we want to have some sort of a base direction on where this is going, but at the last minute, you won’t expect this to go the way you’re thinking. This plot in the MLYU will be the beginning of where there will be multiple groups and heroes will team up and you’ll see this a lot more often, especially when we dive more into future appearances of other Avengers and Marvel characters throughout the MLYU. The Fantastic Four teaming up with the Spider Trio and Jean Grey is very exciting and the finale of this movie will leave you wondering about what this overall story will be. More details will come later within the week, but we will see when the next announcements will occur. Currently, The Idea Maker is at 464 subscribers and the official trailer for LEGO X-Men: The Uncanny Beginning is coming soon at 475 subscribers, while filming the first installment is underway, filming for LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt will be commencing immediately right after X-Men, and then filming for Fantastic Four commences immediately afterwards.
