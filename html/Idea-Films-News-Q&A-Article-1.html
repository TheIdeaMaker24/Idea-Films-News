A LEGO Minifigure

With these past two articles, there has been a few questions that have been risen and over at Idea Films News and The Idea Maker’s YouTube channel, we have decided to have several articles throughout the run that helps answer any questions and even address any theories, rumors, and comments that will appear here and we’ll be looking over fans’ questions, comments, concerns, theories, and more!! We start off with several questions from friend and YouTuber, Ezra Carr376 who asks first:

Ezra Carr376: How long will your LEGO X-Men movie run for?
The Idea Maker: That’s a great question. Since there’s so much story in this script and the actor script is 64 pages long, I don’t wanna assume a short runtime, but maybe around somewhere between 30-50 minutes. Usually the voice actors would provide their voices first and then we’ll film it, but because of the close deadline, it’ll have to be filming first for my scenes that I voice certain characters and have voiceovers for and then I can determine how long the video will be. This is also including the editing, visual effects, and any issues that can occur or need a reshoot but we’re hoping for a smooth production.
Ezra Carr376: How many phases will this new Marvel LEGO Universe be?
The Idea Maker: For that question, I’m not entirely sure about that, because there’s so many villains and so many characters and stories that can be written. I think for now, this saga, which is currently untitled, is gonna be around 3-5 phases since there’s a huge villain I have in mind but I want the build up to also have individual stories that build on other heroes and team ups. I want it to truly feel like a Marvel universe of so many amazing opportunities so the story will keep progressing as it goes.
