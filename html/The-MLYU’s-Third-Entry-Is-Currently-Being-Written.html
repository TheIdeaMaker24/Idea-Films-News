The MLYU's Third Entry Is Currently Being Written

The Marvel Logo

September 8, 2025

Today, we unveil that the third entry in the MLYU is being written now that LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire: Hunt has its script officially written and completed.

“The third entry in the MLYU is going to be a quicker paced movie, as we dive into something that will start showing a recurring theme that will not only go over the course of the first phase of the MLYU, but will also branch into the whole first saga that will take about 3-5 phases to full tell. This is unless there are projects that fans wants to see, then we can adjust the story to fit what the audience wants!”

- The Idea Maker

As we mentioned before, the X-Men will be scattered around Phase 1 of the MLYU and in this story, we have one X-Men involved. To get those interested in the next entry, we will unveil the title and characters involved in this next entry. We call the next entry, LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt.

For this release, we have these following proposed milestones:

  • The official trailer will be released at 525 subscribers.
  • LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt will be released at 550 subscribers.

As for the next upcoming entries in Phase 1 of the Marvel LEGO YouTube Universe, it’ll go as follows:

  • LEGO X-Men: The Uncanny Beginning will be released at 500 subscribers.
  • LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt will be released at 550 subscribers.
  • The third untitled MLYU movie will be released 600 subscribers.
  • The fourth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 700 subscribers.
  • The fifth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 750 subscribers.
  • The sixth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 800 subscribers.
  • The seventh untitled MLYU movie will be released at 850 subscribers.
  • The eight untitled MLYU movie will be released at 1,000 subscribers.

So far, the cast for LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt are:

  • KiiD KAii - Miles Morales/Spider-Man
  • ReelHooperKnowledge - Peter Parker/Spider-Man
  • The Idea Maker - Morbius, Beast, Doctor Strange, Loki
  • Mikeballer24 - Blade
  • ShinGetterWolf1 - Werewolf By Night
  • Mrs. Concept - Wanda Maximoff

This is the confirmed cast so far. There is more casting that we need for a mystery character and an additional member of the Midnight Sons. Yes that’s right! The Midnight Sons will be teaming up with Spider-Man, Miles Morales, and Beast! The team is comprised of Blade, Doctor Strange, Wanda Maximoff, Werewolf By Night, and Loki is their temporary member for story reasons. More details will come later within the week, but we will see when the next casting and crew announcements will occur.
