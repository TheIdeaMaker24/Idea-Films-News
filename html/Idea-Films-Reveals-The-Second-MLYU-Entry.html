Idea Films Reveals The Second MLYU Entry

The Marvel Logo

September 5, 2025

Today, we unveil the next entry after LEGO X-Men: The Uncanny Beginning. Say hello to the next entry in the LEGO Marvel YouTube Universe.

“For the next entry in the MLYU, we will be focusing on another hero that helped shape the Marvel universe, but in this, we will also be introducing a superhero team that has been reincarnated multiple times, but we will be having this particular team be teaming up with Spider-Man. Oh and this is also a dual Spider-Man story. This is a Peter Parker AND Miles Morales story!”

- The Idea Maker

As we mentioned before, the X-Men will be scattered around Phase 1 of the MLYU and in this story, we have one X-Men involved. To get those interested in the next entry, we will unveil the title and characters involved in this next entry. We call the next entry, LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt.

A Prototype Logo for LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt

For this release, we have these following proposed milestones:

  • The official trailer will be released at 525 subscribers.
  • LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt will be released at 550 subscribers.

As for the next upcoming entries in Phase 1 of the Marvel LEGO YouTube Universe, it’ll go as follows:

  • LEGO X-Men: The Uncanny Beginning will be released at 500 subscribers.
  • LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt will be released at 550 subscribers.
  • The third untitled MLYU movie will be released 600 subscribers.
  • The fourth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 700 subscribers.
  • The fifth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 750 subscribers.
  • The sixth untitled MLYU movie will be released at 800 subscribers.
  • The seventh untitled MLYU movie will be released at 850 subscribers.
  • The eight untitled MLYU movie will be released at 1,000 subscribers.

So far, the cast for LEGO Spider-Man: Vampire Hunt are:

  • KiiD KAii - Miles Morales/Spider-Man
  • ReelHooperKnowledge - Peter Parker/Spider-Man
  • The Idea Maker - Morbius, Beast, Doctor Strange, Loki
  • Mikeballer24 - Blade
  • ShinGetterWolf1 - Werewolf By Night
  • * Mrs. Concept - Wanda Maximoff

This is the confirmed cast so far. There is more casting that we need for a mystery character and an additional member of the Midnight Sons. Yes that’s right! The Midnight Sons will be teaming up with Spider-Man, Miles Morales, and Beast! The team is comprised of Blade, Doctor Strange, Wanda Maximoff, Werewolf By Night, and Loki is their temporary member for story reasons. More details will come later within the week, but we will see when the next casting and crew announcements will occur. Currently, The Idea Maker is at 425 subscribers and is the official trailer for LEGO X-Men: The Uncanny Beginning is coming soon at 450 subscribers, while filming the first installment.
