Idea Films Clarify What Universe Number is the MLYU

The Marvel Logo

September 1, 2025

To start off the month, we can officially announce the number that the MLYU is. This comes in to credit writer, ShinGetterWolf1, who thought go the number. Thinking about the number was definitely important and it needed to be significant.

“The universe number for the MLYU is Earth-500. Since we’re beginning this new Marvel universe at 500 subscribers on The Idea Maker YouTube channel, we want it to have meaning and a place within the Marvel universe as its own fan universe. Thanks to ShinGetterWolf1, it definitely feels like another brick has been placed in the bigger universe, pun intended, to be the first building bricks to the next step in this vast universe.”

- The Idea Maker

While there isn’t much to report on for the MLYU, this is a great step towards more established Marvel universe, as we begin to see Earth-500 take shape. As a reminder, the timeline consists of these films so far:

  • The teaser trailer and title reveal will be released at 425 subscribers.
  • The official trailer will be released at 450 subscribers.
  • The official clip will be released at 475 subscribers.
  • LEGO X-Men will be released at 500 subscribers.
